**Datapack Features:**

   * Allows anyone to sit wherever they want! (except for mid-air)

   * To sit down, either sneak 3 times, or type "/trigger Sit"!

   * Customize the datapack by typing "/function sit:admin_info"!

*Survival & Multiplayer friendly*
